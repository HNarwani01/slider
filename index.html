One piece
--by Eiichiro Oda
Action, Adventure, Comedy, Drama, Fantasy, Shounen, Super Power
Gol D. Roger, known as the Pirate King, was feared and respected for his incredible strength and prominence in the Grand Line. However, his capture and execution by the World Government brought about dramatic changes felt throughout the world. In his final moments, Roger disclosed the existence of the ultimate treasure, One Piece. This announcement sparked the era of the Great Age of Pirates, as many sought to fulfill their dreams of discovering this unknown treasure and becoming the Pirate King.
Naruto
-- by Masashi Kishimoto
Action, Adventure, Comedy, Drama, Fantasy, Martial Arts, School, Shounen, Super Power, Supernatural, Demons
Just before Naruto Uzumaki was born, a colossal demon called the Kyuubi, also known as the Nine-Tailed Fox, invaded Konohagakure, the Hidden Leaf Village, causing chaos and destruction. To quell the Kyuubi's fury, the Fourth Hokage, the village leader, gave up his life and entrapped the monstrous creature inside the newborn Naruto. Presently, Naruto is a lively and dense ninja residing in Konohagakure. Excluded by his community because of the Kyuubi within him, Naruto grapples to establish his worth in the village. Despite this, his unyielding aspiration to lead Konohagakure as Hokage draws him towards making new companions and facing dangerous adversaries.
bleach
-- by Tite Kubo
Action, Adventure, Fantasy, Shounen, Super Power, Supernatural, Demons
When his family falls victim to a Hollow attack, Ichigo Kurosaki's life takes an extraordinary turn. Rukia Kuchiki, a Soul Reaper, steps in to protect Ichigo's loved ones, but suffers an injury in the process. To save his family, Ichigo accepts Rukia's offer to inherit her powers and become a Soul Reaper himself. Now tasked with defending their town from the supernatural Hollows, Ichigo teams up with classmates Orihime Inoue, Yasutora Sado, and Uryuu Ishida, each with their own unique abilities. As they work together to navigate their new roles and protect their community, they soon discover that the Hollows are not their only threat.
berserk
--by Kentaro Miura
Action, Adventure, Drama, Fantasy, Horror, Military, Seinen, Supernatural, Demons
Set during a time that very much resembles Europe during the Middle Ages, Berserk is a story of revenge set in the castle town of Midland. Recently, the town has seen the rise of a wicked king, who uses demonic minions to control and victimise his subjects. However, when a lone soldier enters the town calling himself the Black Swordsman and armed to the teeth, many sense that the king's days of unchecked oppression are over. Soon, the Black Swordsman is plying his trade by hunting down the king's evil servants, giving no quarter, and preparing to exact his vengeance on the king.
classroom of the elite
-- by Syougo Kinugasa
Drama, Psychological, School, Suspense
Koudo Ikusei Senior High School is a leading school with state-of-the-art facilities. The students there have the freedom to wear any hairstyle and bring any personal effects they desire. Koudo Ikusei is like a utopia, but the truth is that only the most superior students receive favorable treatment. Kiyotaka Ayanokouji is a student of D-class, which is where the school dumps its "inferior" students in order to ridicule them. For a certain reason, Kiyotaka was careless on his entrance examination, and was put in D-class. After meeting Suzune Horikita and Kikyou Kushida, two other students in his class, Kiyotaka's situation begins to change.
vinland saga
--by Makoto Yukimura
Action, Adventure, Drama, Military, Seinen
As the progeny of a formidable Viking warrior, Thorfinn vows to avenge his father's death at the hands of Askeladd, a commanding mercenary leader. He embarks on a mission to confront Askeladd in combat and infiltrates his group, only to find himself embroiled in a struggle for control of the English throne.
